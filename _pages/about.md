I am currently pursuing a DPhil in Philosophy at Oriel College, University of Oxford, under the supervision of <a href="https://www.nkjones.com" style="text-decoration:none" class="hover-underline-animation">Nicholas K. Jones</a>. Prior to this, I completed an MPhil in Philosophical Theology, under the supervision of <a href="https://www.beaumadisonmount.com" style="text-decoration:none" class="hover-underline-animation">Beau Madison Mount</a>. I also earned a BA in Philosophy from The Chinese University of Hong Kong.

My research interests lie primarily in metaphysics and the philosophy of religion, as well as at their intersection. I'm also interested in Chinese philosophy, particularly the thought of <a href="https://plato.stanford.edu/entries/zhu-xi/" style="text-decoration:none; color:#494e52" class="hover-underline-animation">Zhu Xi</a> and the <a href="https://plato.stanford.edu/entries/zhuangzi/" style="text-decoration:none; color:#494e52" class="hover-underline-animation"><i>Zhuangzi</i></a>.

I'm from Hong Kong.
